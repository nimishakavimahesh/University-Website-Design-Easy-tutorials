World's biggest University

Shinawatra University (SIU) is an international university. Founded in 1999, it was established in response to the current need to educate young people to keep pace with global trends and rapid changes in work culture, thinking and ways of doing things. To achieve these goals, the university places emphasis on the selection of faculty, buildings, dormitories, libraries and communication networks so that learning facilities, including the learning environment, can promote a learning atmosphere; the university currently has students and staffs from more than 30 countries
